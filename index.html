Invitation for Hawaghar Workshop

Sir/Madam,

We are writing to request you to inform your students of the 3-week workshop we are organizing in Kathmandu, starting August 8th, 2017 (Tuesday).

The workshop will last three weeks, and is designed to be massively multi-disciplinary, and will greatly benefit your students of all streams.

We are a group of individuals in our mid-twenties, who have studied and worked in the United States, and in Nepal. Having had a civic-minded, all-encompassing workshop would have improved our connection with the city and country, and gained us a better understanding of our institutions in Nepal function, and how one can work under given constraints. We have organized the workshop to address those concerns – things that we wish we had known, that would have made living and working in Nepal more easy and convenient.

Over the three weeks, we will cover aspects of art (poetry, prose), creative technology (using algorithms to write poetry and generate art), event organization and entrepreneurship, civic activism, and civic technology. Our multi-disciplinary workshop is intended as a supplement to students’ higher education – while colleges like yours produce experts in their field, we want to encourage them to explore fields (art, leadership, technology, civic activism) tangential to what is taught in the classroom. Our aim is to assist you in creating able, responsible citizens. The massively multi-disciplinary approach will give them a greater insight to fields that are unrelated to their own, and encourage them for greater across-the-box approach to problem-solving even in their academic life.

We are confident the workshop will be a very positive experience for your students. The workshop is a great investment, not only for students personally, but also to your college.

We would like to request you to please inform your students about the workshop, in case they are interested in it. Sending school representatives to the workshop on behalf of the college to share the knowledge later would be a gain for you as well as your students also.
